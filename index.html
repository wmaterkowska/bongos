Instructions

  1. Drag elements onto the canvas.
  2. Click a node, then click another to connect them.
  3. Click the same node twice to add a self-loop.
  4. Click empty canvas to cancel a selection.
  5. Drag on empty canvas to select multiple nodes, then drag one of them to move the whole group.
  6. Hold Space and drag to pan the canvas.
  7. Click a line to flip its momentum direction.
  8. Right-click to delete a node or edge.
  9. Each vertex needs exactly 4 legs (amber border = warning).
  10. Toggle Quiz mode to hide the factor cards — click each one (or the canvas momentum lines) to reveal it.
