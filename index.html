Instructions

  1. Drag elements onto the canvas.
  2. Click a node, then click another to connect them.
  3. Click the same node twice to add a self-loop.
  4. Click empty canvas to cancel a selection.
  5. Click a line to flip its momentum direction.
  6. Right-click to delete a node or edge.
  7. Each vertex needs exactly 4 legs (amber border = warning).
